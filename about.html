Hello, I am Mitchell Victoria

Environmental Studies, Masters of Science, based in Santa Cruz County

I am a Santa Cruz native who enjoys the natural beauty of the ocean and surrounding redwood forest. I graduated with a BA in Environmental Studies from UC Santa Cruz in 2019. Currently, I am in my last semester at San Jose State University. This winter, I will have obtained a Masters in Science in Environmental Studies. I have extensive experience with rigorous analysis of scientific methods, observational research, assessing a region's natural resources, and environment. Furthermore, I have also completed ecological field work involving animals. Such as setting up camera traps, small mammal traps, and catching reptiles with herp sticks.
